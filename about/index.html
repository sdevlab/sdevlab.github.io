About SDEV Lab

About

Founded in 2018, SDEV Lab (formely known as ARISE Lab at HGU) has been researching software engineering methodologies to assist developers in building and maintaining their software products correctly and securely. SDEV Lab aims to transform more aspects of software development into structured engineering tasks by fostering collaboration between human developers and automated techniques.
